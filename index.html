  • Safety

    At GetHubby our top priority is the safety of our users. All users are vetted to make sure they are who they claim.

  • Privacy

    We have a strict no-log policy when it comes to user messages and all user data is securely encrypted on military grade servers.

  • Repos

    Looking for a conversation starter? Chat about your repos! Our GitHub integration allows you pick & choose which projects you're most proud of to be displayed on your profile.

Susan and Gordon

Susan & Gordon's Q&A
What did you do for your first date?

Susan: We went to a concert. It was my idea. We'd talked about how we both love Toad the Wet Sprocket so when I heard they were playing I took it as a sign and made the first move.

Susan: Traitor.

Gordon: I know!
