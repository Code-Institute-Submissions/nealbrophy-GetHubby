Why use getHubby?

Safety

At GetHubby our top priority is the safety of our users. All users are vetted to make sure they are who they claim (including a full background check by our SecurityTeam).

Privacy

We have a strict no-log policy when it comes to user messages and all user data is securely encrypted on military grade servers. Additionally, our platform is fully open source and viewable on GitHub.

Community

With over 250,000 members worldwide we're confident there's someone for everyone. If you're a little shy, why not let our digital matchmaker do the work?

Matchmaking

Hunny, our digitalMatchmaker, uses a best-in-class matching-algorithm combined with old-school hand-curation resulting in a 79% success rate.

Repos

Looking for a conversation starter? Chat about your repos! Our GitHub integration allows you pick & choose which projects you're most proud of to be displayed on your profile. Not only does this let you show your dev cred but it's also a great ice breaker!

Hackathon Date

Not great at the online chit chat? Come to one of our Speed-Dating-Meets-Hackathon events and you can get to know other singles while you code together!
